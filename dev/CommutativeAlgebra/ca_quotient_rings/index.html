Quotient Rings of Polynomial Rings

We discuss functionality for working with quotient rings of multivariate polynomial rings which may or may not have an assigned grading.

Constructor

quo — Method
quo(R::MPolyRing, I::MPolyIdeal) -> MPolyQuoRing, Map

Create the quotient ring $R/I$ and return the new ring as well as the projection map $R\rightarrow R/I$.

quo(R::MPolyRing, V::Vector{MPolyElem}) -> MPolyQuoRing, Map

As above, where $I\subset R$ is the ideal generated by the polynomials in $V$.

Examples

julia> R, (x, y) = PolynomialRing(QQ, ["x", "y"])
(Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y over Rational Field, fmpq_mpoly[x, y])

julia> Q, _ = quo(R, ideal(R, [x^2-y^3, x-y]))
(Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y over Rational Field by ideal generated by: x^2 - y^3, x - y, Map from
Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y over Rational Field to Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y over Rational Field by ideal generated by: x^2 - y^3, x - y defined by a julia-function with inverse
)

julia> typeof(x)
fmpq_mpoly

julia> Q, q = quo(R, ideal(R, [x^2-y^3, x-y]))
(Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y over Rational Field by ideal generated by: x^2 - y^3, x - y, Map from
Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y over Rational Field to Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y over Rational Field by ideal generated by: x^2 - y^3, x - y defined by a julia-function with inverse
)

julia> q
Map from
Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y over Rational Field to Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y over Rational Field by ideal generated by: x^2 - y^3, x - y defined by a julia-function with inverse

julia> q(x)
x

julia> typeof(q(x))
MPolyQuoElem{fmpq_mpoly}

julia> S, (x, y, z) = GradedPolynomialRing(QQ, ["x", "y", "z"]);

julia> P, p = quo(S, ideal(S, [x^2*z-y^3, x-y]))
(Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y, z over Rational Field graded by
  x -> [1]
  y -> [1]
  z -> [1] by ideal generated by: x^2*z - y^3, x - y, Map from
Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y, z over Rational Field graded by
  x -> [1]
  y -> [1]
  z -> [1] to Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y, z over Rational Field graded by
  x -> [1]
  y -> [1]
  z -> [1] by ideal generated by: x^2*z - y^3, x - y defined by a julia-function with inverse
)
source
Note

In each case, with or without an assigned grading, the return type of quo is a subtype of MPolyQuo.

Note

In Oscar, elements of quotient rings are not necessarily reduced with regard to their modulus. Operations involving Gröbner basis computations may lead to partial reductions. Full reductions are achieved by explicitly computing normal forms. See the corresponding section on Groebner bases and normal forms.

Data Associated to Quotient Rings of Polynomial Rings

Basic Data

If Q=R/I is the quotient of a multivariate polynomial ring R modulo an ideal I of R, then

  • base_ring(Q) refers to R,
  • modulus(Q) to I,
  • gens(Q) to the generators of Q, and
  • ngens(Q) to the number of these generators.
Examples
julia> R, (x, y, z) = PolynomialRing(QQ, ["x", "y", "z"])
(Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y, z over Rational Field, fmpq_mpoly[x, y, z])

julia> Q, _ = quo(R, ideal(R, [y-x^2, x-z^3]))
(Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y, z over Rational Field by ideal generated by: -x^2 + y, x - z^3, Map from
Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y, z over Rational Field to Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y, z over Rational Field by ideal generated by: -x^2 + y, x - z^3 defined by a julia-function with inverse
)

julia> base_ring(Q)
Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x, y, z over Rational Field

julia> modulus(Q)
ideal generated by: -x^2 + y, x - z^3

julia> gens(Q)
3-element Array{MPolyQuoElem{fmpq_mpoly},1}:
 x
 y
 z

julia> ngens(Q)
3

Tests on Quotient Rings of Polynomial Rings

Reducedness Test

isreduced — Method
isreduced(Q::MPolyQuo)

Given a quotient ring Q = R/I, return true if Q is reduced, false otherwise.

CAVEAT: The function computes the radical of I. This may take some time.

Examples

julia> R, (x,) = PolynomialRing(QQ, ["x"])
(Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x over Rational Field, fmpq_mpoly[x])

julia> Q, _ = quo(R, ideal(R, [x^4]))
(Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x over Rational Field by ideal generated by: x^4, Map from
Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x over Rational Field to Quotient of Multivariate Polynomial Ring in x over Rational Field by ideal generated by: x^4 defined by a julia-function with inverse
)

julia> isreduced(Q)
false

Ideals in Quotient Rings of Polynomial Rings

Constructors

ideal — Method
ideal(Q::MPolyQuo{T}, V::Vector{T}) where T <: MPolyElem

Given a (graded) quotient ring Q=R/I and a vector V of (homogeneous) polynomials in R, create the ideal of Q which is generated by the images of the entries of V.

ideal(Q::MPolyQuo{T}, V::Vector{MPolyQuoElem{T}}) where T <: MPolyElem

Given a (graded) quotient ring Q and a vector V of (homogeneous) elements of Q, create the ideal of Q which is generated by the entries of V.

MPolyQuoIdeal(y^2)

julia> J = ideal(A, [x])
MPolyQuoIdeal(x)

julia> intersect(I,J)
MPolyQuoIdeal(x*y)
